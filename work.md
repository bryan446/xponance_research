So unique aspects I want to explore:

I want to leverage the actual exposure of active managers employing these factors for stock selection to set region and sector limits for the new indices.
I want to understand the difference in outcomes between a multi-factor ranking approach vs a 2 step approach. A multi-factor ranking would just use a weighted average the Z scores of a companies Value and Quality scores. A 2 step approach may define a threshold based on value (cheapest 1/3 of the universe) and then apply a multi-factor quality model (or the reverse). Ideally our approach will mirror the practice of active managers.
